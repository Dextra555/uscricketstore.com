US Cricket Store

Promoting Cricket Across America

US Cricket Store promotes cricket across America through our partnership with CricKingdom Cricket Academy by Rohit Sharma. We offer top-notch training programs endorsed by international cricket boards, guided by legends like Rohit Sharma and Dhawal Kulkarni. At US Cricket Store, our mission is to elevate cricket in America by fostering talent and passion for the sport.
